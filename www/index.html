About EmoMap

EmoMap (Emotional Mapping) is part of a research project at the Vienna University of Technology, Austria, which aims to map people's affective/emotional responses towards different environment. While you report your feelings, your mobile phone's GPS estimates your current location. Your answers and location information are then anonymously and securely sent to our database. The data contributed are solely used for our academic research. We aim to investigate how people affectively response to different environments. For more information regarding EmoMap, please visit cartography.tuwien.ac.at.
